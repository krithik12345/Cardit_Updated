About Us

Learn more about our mission, our team, and how we're revolutionizing the way debaters get their evidence and practice.

Our Mission

At Card It, our mission is to provide debaters with the most efficient and user-friendly platform to gather and organize evidence. We believe in making research accessible, efficient, and impactful. We also believe in allowing debaters all over the world access to proper practice and community forums that facilitate enriching communication.
